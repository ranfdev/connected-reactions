Description This is a turn based online game in which the purpose is to "eat" the enemy color, by exploding the atoms of your color.
Rules You have a grid where you can place some protons. Your protons have a unique color. You can add more protons to a cell, until the cell explodes. When the cell explodes, the protons will expand 1 in the upper cell, 1 in the bottom cell, 1 in the right cell, and 1 in the left cell. A cell explodes when: the protons are more than 1 and are in one of the corners. the protons are more than 2 and touch one of the borders. the protons are more than 3 and are somewhere else.
